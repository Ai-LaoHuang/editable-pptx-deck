# Spring Tea Launch Editorial Redesign

## Direction

Chinese commercial proposal deck rebuilt in an editorial magazine style:
architecture photography, calm typographic grid, low-saturation palette,
generous negative space, thin rules, and fewer card-like modules.

## Visual System

- Canvas: 1280 x 720
- Palette: warm paper, limestone, ink black, muted sage, brushed brass
- Typography: Microsoft YaHei / Arial; large editorial titles, small captions
- Layout: strict margins, column grids, image-led opening and section pages
